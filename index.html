About Us

Here at Golden Geese, we create top quality toys. Our innovative designs are safe, exciting, and even collectible! Check out our latest launch, the Golden Geese Kinder Egg Toy.

What should you be looking out for in your next Kinder Egg? The new Golden Geese Kinder Toy! Assemble these 3 birds into a V formation and be amazed at the speed and grace of the flock. This toy is guaranteed to travel at least 2.5m at an unrivaled speed. The toy was printed and assembled here at the University of Guelph by top engineers.

For a limited time, you can collect the special Canada 150 edition birds! This flock sports a beautiful design on its back – proudly representing Canada.
